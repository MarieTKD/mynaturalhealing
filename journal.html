The Bad and Ugly

The Seignalet diet is an hypotoxic diet. The goal being to heal your gut so toxins won't escape and create inflammation.
Food to avoid:

  • Milk products from all animals (cream, butter, yogurts...)
  • Most cereals: wheat, rye, barley, oats (rice, sesame, quinoa, tapioca and buckwheat are allowed).
  • refined oils
  • refined white salt
  • white sugar, high fructose corn syrup
  • Reduce the consumption of red meat in favor of white meat, but keep meat consumption to 70gr, maximum twice a day. Avoid bacon, ham, sausage
  • Avoid industrially prepared food as much as you can, fries and chips

These aliments produce toxins in your body, mostly glycotoxins, responsible for a lot of chronic disease.
Eat raw food whenever possible. Low temperature cooking is very important to avoid the creation of glycotoxins as well, especially for the meat. Cooking in a liquid helps, so stew should be fine. Slow-cooker would be useful. Avoid roasting, grilling, frying.

Those are the basis of the diet, the rules I will follow. I avoided going in details since I'm not a health professional, but Jacqueline Lagace's book explains everything in details.
